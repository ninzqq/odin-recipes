Lasagna

Picture of lasagna dish

Minced meat, lasagna "plates", egg, tomato sauce etc

Ingredients

  • Minced meat
  • Lasagna plates
  • Tomato sauce (not ketshup)
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Get plates ready
  3. Place meat and plates one after another as layers
  4. Add tomato sauce after each layer
